Hedging Basics — How to Secure Profits & Limit Losses

Hedging is a fundamental risk-management tool in football betting. This article explains what hedging is, when to apply it, and how it helps you lock in profits or reduce downside.

Key takeaways

  • What is hedging? — placing a second bet to offset risk from the first.
  • When to hedge? — late market moves, cash-out alternatives, or high-variance spots.
  • Why hedge? — security of profit, reduced volatility, improved bankroll stability.

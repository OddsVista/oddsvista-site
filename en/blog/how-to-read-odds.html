How to Read Odds in Football Betting

Odds tell you both the potential payout and the implied probability of an outcome. This guide covers the three main formats — decimal, fractional, and American — so you can read any odds board with confidence.

Key takeaways

  • Decimal odds — standard in Europe (e.g., 2.50 means €10 → €25 return).
  • Fractional odds — common in UK (e.g., 5/2 means €10 → €35 return).
  • American odds — positive (+150) or negative (−200) values indicating profit per stake.
  • Implied probability — convert odds to % chance for smarter betting decisions.

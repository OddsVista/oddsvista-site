Bankroll Management 101 — Smart Betting Basics

Managing your bankroll is the foundation of responsible football betting. This guide shows how to separate betting funds from daily money, apply disciplined staking, and handle variance with confidence.

Key takeaways

  • Always keep betting money separate from everyday expenses.
  • Use flat or percentage staking to control risk of ruin.
  • Stick to your plan — discipline beats emotions.

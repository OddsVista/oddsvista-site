Impact of Player Injuries on Football Betting

Injuries can shift odds dramatically, but not all absences are equal. Understanding which players truly matter — and how markets react — is key to making informed decisions.

Key takeaways

  • Star players vs squad depth — not every injury has the same impact.
  • Market overreactions — public betting often exaggerates injury news.
  • Timing matters — early info creates opportunities before odds adjust.

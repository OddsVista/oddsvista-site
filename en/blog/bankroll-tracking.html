Bankroll Tracking — How to Monitor Your Betting Balance

Keeping records of your bets is as important as making predictions. With proper bankroll tracking, you understand variance, spot leaks in your strategy, and build long-term discipline.

Key takeaways

  • Record every bet — results, odds, stake, and outcome.
  • Use tools — spreadsheets or apps keep discipline consistent.
  • Review monthly — analyze ROI, variance, and adjust strategy.

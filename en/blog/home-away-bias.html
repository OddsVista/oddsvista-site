Home vs Away Bias in Football Betting

Bookmakers and bettors often overvalue home advantage. This article explains the psychology and statistics behind the home/away bias and shows how to exploit it for smarter betting.

Key takeaways

  • Home bias — crowds and familiarity give home teams inflated odds.
  • Away undervaluation — public underestimates traveling teams, creating value opportunities.
  • Smart approach — evaluate stats beyond location and look for inefficiencies.

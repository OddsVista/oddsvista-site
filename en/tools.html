Implied Probability & Odds Converter Basics

Tip: Decimal → implied% = 100 / decimal. American +150 ⇒ 2.50; −120 ⇒ 1.833…

Expected Value (EV) Long-run

EV = p × (odds − 1) − (1 − p). Positive EV doesn’t mean guaranteed wins.

Kelly Criterion Risk

Kelly full = (bp − q)/b where b = (odds−1), p = win prob, q = 1−p. Many use 0.25–0.50 (fractional Kelly).

Parlay / Accumulator Multi-leg

Total decimal = product of legs. Implied% = 100 / total.

Bookmaker Margin (Overround) 1X2

Margin = (Σ(100/odds)) − 100. Lower margin is generally better for bettors.

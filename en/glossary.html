Glossary

Quick, plain-English definitions for football analytics and betting. Educational only; no guarantees. Always follow local laws.

A B C D E F I K L M O P R S V X
Accumulator (Acca) / Parlay
Combined bet of multiple selections; all must win for a payout.
Arbitrage
Opposing bets across books to lock a small theoretical profit. Practical constraints include limits, delays, and rules.
Asian Handicap
Handicap market that uses half/quarter lines (e.g., -0.25, +0.75) to reduce or remove the draw.
Bankroll
Total funds allocated for betting; manage to survive variance.
Closing Line Value (CLV)
How your price compares to the market’s final odds; a benchmark of long-term skill.
Decimal Odds
European odds format showing total return per 1 unit (including stake).
Edge
Your estimated advantage vs bookmaker price.
Expected Value (EV)
Average outcome of a bet over the long run; positive EV is the target.
Fractional Odds
UK format showing profit relative to stake (e.g., 5/2).
Implied Probability
Probability derived from odds after accounting for margin (overround).
In-Play / Live Betting
Placing bets while a match is ongoing; consider latency, momentum, and market delays.
Kelly Criterion
Stake sizing formula based on edge; fractional Kelly reduces volatility.
Line Shopping
Comparing odds across books to secure the best available price.
Moneyline / 1X2
Winner market (home/draw/away) or straight win in some regions.
Over/Under (Totals)
Bet on total goals relative to a line (e.g., 2.5).
Push
Stake returned when result equals the line (e.g., AH 0, total 2.0).
Return on Investment (ROI)
Profit relative to total staked; evaluate over a large sample.
Stake
Amount placed on a selection; tie to bankroll rules.
Value Bet
When true probability exceeds the implied probability from odds.
Variance
Natural randomness causing short-term swings independent of edge.
xG (Expected Goals)
Model estimate of chance quality based on shot location/type and context.
xGA
Expected goals against; conceded chance quality.
xPoints (xPts)
Expected points calculated from chance quality rather than final score.

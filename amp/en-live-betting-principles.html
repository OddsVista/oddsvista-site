Live Betting Principles

  • Latency: TV delays, streams, and book delays → size down.
  • Priors: keep your pre-match model; update with shots/xThreat/cards.
  • Suspensions: know the rules for VAR, pens, corners, red cards.
  • Liquidity: spreads fill faster than exotics; avoid thin tails.

In-play mistakes compound quickly. Tighten units and review execution logs.

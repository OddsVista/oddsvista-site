xG vs Goals

  • Goals = outcome; xG = process & chance quality.
  • Large gaps (xG ≫ goals) often regress; check shot locations & blocks.
  • Finishing talent exists, but smaller than fans think — sample size matters.

Use both: price forward-looking with xG; evaluate luck with Goals−xG.

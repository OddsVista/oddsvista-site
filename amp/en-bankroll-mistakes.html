Bankroll Mistakes

  • Chasing after losses; doubling stakes to “get even”.
  • Letting bet size creep with short-term wins.
  • Ignoring fees, FX, and withdrawal frictions.
  • Overexposure to long parlays for “lottery” payoffs.
Fix: lock unit size, set loss limits, review weekly, and track CLV.
